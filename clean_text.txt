Since quality enhancement is a continuous process, the IQAC will become a part of an institution’s system and work towards realizing the goals of quality enhancement and sustenance. Vision To be an institute of repute, providing globally competent and socially sensitive professionals Mission • To provide state-of-art infrastructure, processes, centers of excellence and human resources. • To inculcate qualities of leadership, professionalism, corporate understanding and executive competence. • To imbibe and enhance human values, ethics and morals amongst students. • To facilitate industry connects, provide skills to become globally competitive professionals. Goals • To develop a system for conscious, consistent and catalytic improvement in the performance of institutions. • To promote measures for institutional functioning towards quality enhancement through internalization of quality culture and institutionalization of best practices. Strategies. IQAC shall evolve mechanisms and procedures for a) Ensuring timely, efficient and progressive performance of academic, administrative and financial tasks b) The relevance and quality of academic and research programmes c) Equitable access to and affordability of academic programmes for various sections of society d) Optimization and integration of modern methods of teaching and learning e) The credibility of evaluation procedures f) Ensuring the adequacy, maintenance and proper allocation of support structure and services g) Sharing of research findings and networking with other institutions in India and abroad. Functions of IQAC • Development and application of quality benchmarks / parameters for the various academic and administrative activities • Dissemination of information on the various quality parameters of higher education • Organization of workshops, seminars on quality related themes and promotion of quality circles • Documentation of the various programmes / activities leading to quality improvement. • Preparation of the Annual Quality Assurance Report (AQAR) to be submitted to NAAC based on the quality parameters. • Development and maintenance of institutional database through ERP/LMS for the purpose of maintaining/enhancing the institutional quality • Arrangement for feedback response from students, parents and other stakeholders on quality-related institutional processes • Conduction of Annual Academic Audit of various departments in the institute. Benefits • Ensure heightened level of clarity and focus in institutional functioning towards quality • enhancement. • Ensure internalization of the quality culture. • Ensure enhancement and integration among the various activities of the institution and institutionalize good practices. • Provide a sound basis for decision-making to improve institutional functioning.

The ideological orientation of the NSS is inspired by the ideals of Mahatma Gandhi. Very appropriately, the motto of NSS is “NOT ME, BUT YOU”. An NSS volunteer places the ‘community’ before ‘self'. The social activities aim to include social welfare in students, and to provide service to society without bias. NSS volunteers work to ensure that everyone who is needy gets help to enhance their standard of living and lead life of dignity. The students actively participated in events conducted on NSS units. OBJECTIVES OF NSS: NSS aims at developing the following qualities/ competencies among the volunteers: 1. To understand the community in which the NSS volunteers work 2. To understand themselves in relation to their community 3. To identify the needs and problems of the community and involve themselves in problem-solving exercise 4. To develop among themselves a sense of social and civic responsibility 5. To utilize their knowledge in finding practical solutions to individual and community problems 6. To develop competence required for group-living and sharing of responsibilities 7. To gain skills in mobilizing community participation 8. To acquire leadership qualities and democratic values 9. To develop capacity to meet emergencies and natural disasters 10. To practice national integration and social harmony NSS attempts to establish meaningful linkages between ‘Campus and Community’, ‘College and Village’ and ‘Knowledge and Action’. MOTTO - "NOT ME BUT YOU" The Motto of NSS "Not Me But You", reflects the essence of democratic living and upholds the need for self-less service. NSS helps the students development & appreciation to other person's point of view and also show consideration towards other living beings. The philosophy of the NSS is a good doctrine in this motto, which underlines on the belief that the welfare of an individual is ultimately dependent on the welfare of the society as a whole and therefore, the NSS volunteers shall strive for the well-being of the society. ADVANTAGES / BENEFIT OF JOINING NSS: • Personality Development • Incentive • Preference in Admission / Employment • National Integration camp, Adventure Programme, State level as well as National Level programme • It helps in acquiring leadership qualities and democratic attitude. • It provides diversified opportunities to students in colleges and universities to develop their personality through community service. • It developer a sense of involvement in the task of nation building. • Volunteer’s interaction with the slum dwellers and villagers expose them to the realities of life and bring about a change in their social perception. • It helps in developing positive attitude, self-confidence, courage & patience. • It helps in developing his / her skill to perform certain jobs, quality of an organizer an Administrator and development of personality as a whole. • He/she gets opportunities to see the community closely and thus gets an experience of human nature in relation to his / her environment. • Gets the opportunity to meet the people from different walks of life. • He/she gets the opportunity to participate in various National / State level programme such as National Integration, Motivational Lamps, value oriented self-development camps, Adventure camps, workshops. Youth exhibition, cultural programmes etc. • A National service scheme contingent of 100 (boys and girls) participants selected after a rigorous test participates in the Republic day parade camp for 3 weeks at New Delhi in the month of January every year. • NSS volunteer completing 240 hours of regular community service in a period of two years is to be issued an NSS certificate by the college. ENROLLMENT POLICIES Enrolment into the National Service Scheme is on a voluntary basis. Students, both boys and girls can join but they should not be a NCC Cadet. The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) at GL Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management offers B.Tech (4 Years), B.Tech (Hindi) (4 Years), and M.Tech (2 Years) programs. All programs are approved by AICTE and affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU).
Established in 2005, the department was founded with a strong vision to support the rapid growth of the IT industry and to fulfill the global demand for skilled and dynamic software engineers. Over the years, particularly during the technological advancements of the last decade, the department has achieved significant milestones and is now regarded as one of the premier departments of the institute.
The Computer Science & Engineering Department is headed by Dr. Sansar Singh Chauhan, who serves as the Head of the Department (HOD), CSE. Under his academic leadership and guidance, the department has continuously strengthened its teaching methodologies, research initiatives, and industry-oriented learning practices.

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering with specialization in Artificial Intelligence (CSE–AI) was established in 2020 with the objective of developing future-ready professionals in the rapidly evolving field of Artificial Intelligence. The department blends strong computer science fundamentals with advanced AI technologies to meet industry and research demands.
The department is headed by Dr. Sanjeev Kumar Pippal, Head of the Department, CSE–AI, who serves as the Head of the Department (HOD), whose leadership and academic vision have been instrumental in strengthening AI-focused education, research, and innovation.

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering Data Science(CSDS) focuses on academic excellence, research innovation, and strong industry collaboration in the domain of data-driven technologies. The department aims to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to analyze, interpret, and utilize data effectively across diverse sectors.
The department is headed by Dr. Harikesh Singh, who serves as the Head of the Department (HOD), CSE–Data Science, who provides academic leadership and guidance in curriculum development, research initiatives, and industry-oriented learning.

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) at GL Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management offers a B.Tech (4 Years) program approved by AICTE and affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU). Established in 2005, the department has been a cornerstone of technical excellence since the inception of the institute.
The department is headed by Dr. Satyendra Sharma, who serves as the Head of the Department (HOD), ECE, whose academic leadership and experience have significantly contributed to curriculum development, research initiatives, and industry engagement.
The department provides students with strong foundations in wireless communication, VLSI design, embedded systems, antenna design, and advanced communication technologies using modern software tools. Emphasis is placed on hands-on learning, technical skill development beyond the curriculum, and preparing students to meet global technological challenges.

The Department of Mechanical Engineering(ME) at GL Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management was established in 2005 and offers a B.Tech (4 Years) program approved by AICTE and affiliated to AKTU. The department plays a vital role in developing technically sound and industry-ready mechanical engineers.
The department is headed by Dr. Vishwa Ratna Mishra, who serves as the Head of the Department (HOD), Mechanical Engineering, whose guidance has strengthened academic rigor, research orientation, and practical learning.
The department focuses on enriching students with technical, analytical, quantitative, ethical, and problem-solving skills. Supported by a team of experienced faculty from premier institutions, the department emphasizes modern manufacturing, thermal sciences, design, and computational research to meet industry and societal needs.

The Department of Information Technology(IT) at GL Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management was established in 2007 with a vision to develop competent IT professionals capable of meeting global industry demands. The department offers a B.Tech (4 Years) program approved by AICTE and affiliated to AKTU.
The department is headed by Dr. P. C. Vashist, who serves as the Head of the Department (HOD), Information Technology, who provides strong academic leadership and promotes innovation-driven learning.
The department focuses on scientific research, application programming, software engineering, and emerging IT tools and technologies. Well-equipped laboratories and a research-oriented environment enable students to achieve professional excellence and contribute effectively to industry and society.
